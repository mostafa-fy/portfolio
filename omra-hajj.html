Project Detail

Umrah allowance is a wensite that provides allowance service for Hajj and Umrah according to Islamic law The site and application allows self-service to request the Hajj or Umrah service or request the distribution of water or any other service within the Grand Mosque in Mecca A special section for offices that allows managing and registering orders from customers and benefiting from discount coupons A special section for service executors, where the executor executes the request, provides implementation updates such as photos or videos, and receives the service fee at the end
